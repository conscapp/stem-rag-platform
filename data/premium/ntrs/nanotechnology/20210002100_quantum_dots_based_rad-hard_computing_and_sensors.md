# Quantum dots based rad-hard computing and sensors

## Summary

Quantum dots based rad-hard computing and sensors

## Abstract

Quantum Dots (QDs) are solid state structures made of semiconductors or metals that confine a small number of electrons into a small space.  The confinement of electrons is achieved by the placement of some insulating material(s) around a central, well conducting region.  Thus, they can ve viewed as artificial atoms.
