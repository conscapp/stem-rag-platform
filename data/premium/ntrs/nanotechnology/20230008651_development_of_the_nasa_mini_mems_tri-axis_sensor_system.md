# Development of the NASA Mini/MEMS Tri-Axis Sensor System

## Summary

Development of the NASA Mini/MEMS Tri-Axis Sensor System

## Abstract

This manuscript details the development of a reduced   volume   attitude   sensor   developed   at NASA.   This   new   sensor   uses   MEMs   based accelerometers   to   reduce   the   volume   when compared to current sensors used in wind-tunnel models.  Our team has designed, fabricated, and evaluated   the   Mini/MEMs   Tri-Axis   Sensor System (MTASS).   The   current   MTASS   design accommodates three tactical-grade accelerometers into the volume of a single axis accelerometer   that   is   currently   used.   This arrangement allows for the estimation of model pitch and roll angles. Static testing of the MTASS demonstrated angle accuracies within ±0.01° for both pitch and roll.
